I'm a Data Analyst with 10+ years of professional experience transforming complex, messy data into clear insights that drive strategic decisions. Continuously deepening expertise in Power BI, DAX, SQL, and data visualization through 36 certifications and hands-on portfolio projects. Collaborative, detail-oriented, and fully bilingual in English and Portuguese — ready to bring analytical clarity to your team.
